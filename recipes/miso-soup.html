Miso Soup

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup that starts with a dashi stock and is flavored with miso paste.

Did you know you can make restaurant-worthy miso soup in the comfort of your own home? Well, you can! You just need a good recipe â€” and that's where we come in. This miso soup recipe is bold, flavorful, and easy to make with just five ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi granules and miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Bring the water and dashi granules to a boil.

  2. Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste.

  3. Stir in the tofu and green onions.

  4. Simmer and serve.
